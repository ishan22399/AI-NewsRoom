Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my points?

Your points are automatically tracked in your profile. You can view your current points and level on this rewards page or in your account dashboard.

Do points expire?

Points are valid for 12 months from the date they were earned. We'll notify you before any points are about to expire.

How do I redeem my rewards?

Simply click on the "Redeem" button next to the reward you want. The points will be deducted from your account and you'll receive instructions on how to access your reward.

Can I gift my points to someone else?

Not at the moment, but we're working on adding this feature in the future!
